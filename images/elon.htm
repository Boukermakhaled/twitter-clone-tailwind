Tesla Tries to Revive Elon Musk’s Big Payday After Court Rejected It

Multibillion-dollar compensation deal was previously struck down in Delaware court; Tesla also moves to incorporate in Texas

By Alyssa Lukpat
, Theo Francis
 and Denny Jacob
Updated April 17, 2024 5:32 pm ET

The fight over Elon Musk’s pay is back on.

Tesla’s TSLA -1.95%decrease; red down pointing triangle board asked shareholders to again vote in favor of its CEO’s multibillion-dollar pay package. The board and shareholders approved the pay proposal in 2018, but a Delaware court in January struck it down because it said the approval process was “deeply flawed.”
